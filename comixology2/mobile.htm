Mobile Access

Connecting Ubooquity to your mobile device

While this page works on mobile devices, the best way to read these comics is via a dedicated app.

Below is how to read these comics on both Android and iOS devices.
Chunky Comic Reader - iPad
This requires the Pro version, which is unlocked with a $4 in-app purchase.

To add the feed, tap the cloud button at the top.


Edit then tap the + next to Calibre / Ubooquity / OPDS .



Server:
Username/Password: Use your logins from here.



The All comics list is massive, I'd recommend using the Folders entry instead (and then tap the pin to bookmark it).


You can then download comics by tapping the down arrow next to the issue, or the All at the bottom (to download all in the visible list).
Challenger Comics Viewer / Chrome - Android
While Challenger also supports OPDS feeds, like Chunky, the support isn't quite as smooth/doesn't support larger comic databases. Instead, I suggest the following:



In Challenger, choose the Add Folder option.






Choose your Internal SD card.






Click the Download folder and hit Select.


Now open this page in your browser (Chrome should work best), choose whatever comic you'd like to read and tap Download.

Go back to Challenger, go to your Library and hit Refresh.


Voila, comics!
